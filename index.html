How to Play Tic-Tac-Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe is a two-player game, where one player plays with 'X' and the other with 'O'. Players take turns choosing a space on the 3x3 board. The goal is to get three of your symbols in a row. This can be horizontal, vertical or diagonal. If all squares are filled and no one has won, the game ends in a draw.
